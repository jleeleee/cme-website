What We Do

Teaching

Selected through a strict audition process, our tutors consist of only the most talented and passionate youth musicians in our community.

Learning

Our unique class structures allow students to learn in a group environment while recieving personalized attention from our tutors.

Performing

We hold recitals for our students at the end of each season. In addition, our tutors frequently perform at local charity events to give back to our community.
