Your Responsibilities


    As a tutor you will be held responsible for:

    Focusing on the musical developements of up to two students in a class
    Promoting good posture and practice habits
    Making sure students are enthusiastic about learning and playing music

Requirements


    Must play a string instrument
    Must be in high school
    Must be receiving private lessons either or have received them in the past
    Must be able to attend every session on Saturdays from 4-5:30 P.M
    Must be able to attend the end-of-season recitals P.M

Audition Requirements

(All Instruments)

    3 octave C, F, A major scales.
    (2 slurred, 2 separate ascending; 4 slurred descending)
    1 Prepared solo that best demonstrates your ability.
    (please bring 2 copies: 1 for the judges, 1 for yourself)
    Prepared excerpt - First 13 measures from the first movement of Mozart's Symphony no.25.
    Violin |   Viola  |   Cello
